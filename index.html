What We Do 我們的角色

We provide comprehensive, one-stop financial planning services covering insurance, investment, and retirement planning, helping our clients achieve long-term financial goals.

We are licensed by the Insurance Authority (IA), the Securities and Futures Commission (SFC), and the Mandatory Provident Fund Schemes Authority (MPFA).

We are a member of the FWD Group. 我們提供全面及專業的一站式理財策劃服務，涵蓋保險、投資及退休規劃，致力協助客戶實現長遠財務目標。

本公司持有保險業監管局（IA）、證券及期貨事務監察委員會（SFC）及強制性公積金計劃管理局（MPFA）牌照。

本公司為富衛（FWD）集團旗下成員。
