About FWD FP關於 FWD FP

FWD Financial Planning Limited is a member of the FWD Group, a pan-Asian life and health insurance group serving approximately 30 million customers across 10 markets.

We are a rapidly expanding brokerage firm holding Insurance Authority (IA), Securities and Futures Commission (SFC) and Mandatory Provident Fund (MPF) licenses, providing a professional platform for holistic financial planning services. 富衛財務策劃有限公司為富衛（FWD）集團旗下成員。富衛集團為一家泛亞洲壽險及健康保險機構，於 10 個市場服務約 3,000 萬名客戶。

本公司為一間迅速發展的經紀公司，持有保險業監管局（IA）、證券及期貨事務監察委員會（SFC）及強制性公積金（MPF）相關牌照，提供全面及專業的理財策劃服務平台。
