Who we are關於我們

FWD Financial Planning Limited is a member of the FWD Group (1828.HK), a pan-Asian life and health insurance business that serves more than 38 million customers across 10 markets.

We are a rapidly expanding insurance brokerage and financial advisory company, providing a professional platform for holistic financial planning services.

We are licensed by:

  • the Insurance Authority (IA) (Licence No. FB1177) for General and Long Term Business (including Linked Long Term Business);
  • the Securities and Futures Commission (SFC) (CE No. AFG549) for Type 1 (Dealing in Securities) and Type 4 (Advising on Securities); and
  • the Mandatory Provident Fund Schemes Authority (MPFA) (Registration No. IC000187) as a Principal Intermediary.
富衛財務策劃有限公司香港為泛亞洲人壽及健康保險公司富衛集團（1828.HK）旗下公司。富衛集團服務約4,000萬名客戶，業務遍及亞洲十個市場。

本公司為一間迅速發展的持牌保險經紀及理財策劃公司，提供專業平台，致力發展全面的理財策劃服務。

本公司持有以下監管牌照：

  • 保險業監管局（IA）（牌照號碼：FB1177），可從事一般業務及長期業務（包括相連長期業務）；
  • 證券及期貨事務監察委員會（SFC）（中央編號：AFG549），可從事第1類（證券交易）及第4類（就證券提供意見）受規管活動；及
  • 強制性公積金計劃管理局（MPFA）（註冊編號：IC000187），為主事中介人。
