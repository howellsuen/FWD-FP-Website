Career Path 事業發展路徑

FWD Financial Planning offers a clear and structured career pathway for financial advisers at different stages of their professional journey. FWD 財務策劃為不同階段的理財顧問提供清晰而有系統的事業發展路徑。

Financial Planning Advisor 理財策劃顧問

Build core advisory capabilities through client servicing, financial reviews and continuous professional development. Fresh graduates and IANG applicants are welcomed. 透過客戶服務、財務檢視及持續專業發展，建立穩固的顧問基礎。歡迎應屆畢業生及 IANG 人士加入。

Manager 經理

Develop people management skills, support junior advisers and expand client relationship management responsibilities. 發展人員管理能力，支援初級顧問，並擴展客戶關係管理職能。

Director 總監

Advance into senior advisory practice and leadership responsibilities, with greater strategic and team oversight, including opportunities for override incentives. 晉身高級顧問及領導層角色，負責更高層次的策略及團隊管理，並享有相關管理獎勵機會。

Executive Director 執行總監

Lead and grow advisory teams through business development, talent recruitment and team expansion, while mentoring future leaders and supporting long‑term growth through team‑based incentives. 透過業務拓展、人才招募及團隊發展，領導及壯大顧問團隊，同時培育未來領袖，並以團隊導向的激勵機制支持長遠發展。

Across all levels, advisers benefit from clear progression towards management roles, performance-based recognition and opportunities such as Million Dollar Round Table (MDRT) qualification. 各職級均設有清晰的管理晉升機制、以表現為本的認可制度，以及如百萬圓桌會（MDRT）等專業成就機會。
