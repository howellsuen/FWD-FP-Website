Why FWD Financial Planning 為何選擇富衛財務策劃

Member of FWD Group富衛集團旗下成員

FWD Financial Planning Limited is a member of the FWD Group (1828.HK), a pan-Asian life and health insurance business that serves more than 38 million customers across 10 markets. 富衛財務策劃有限公司香港為泛亞洲人壽及健康保險公司富衛集團（1828.HK）旗下公司。富衛集團服務約4,000萬名客戶，業務遍及亞洲十個市場。

Licensed Platform專業持牌平台

We are a rapidly expanding insurance brokerage and financial advisory company holding Insurance Authority (IA), Securities and Futures Commission (SFC), and Mandatory Provident Fund (MPF) licenses, providing a professional platform for holistic financial planning services. 本公司為一間迅速發展的持牌保險經紀及理財策劃公司，持有保險業監管局（IA）、證券及期貨事務監察委員會（SFC）及強制性公積金（MPF）相關牌照，致力提供全面及專業的理財策劃服務平台。

Holistic Financial Planning Solutions全面理財策劃方案

Our advisors are empowered to deliver professional, suitable, and goal‑oriented financial planning advice, supported by a customer‑centric philosophy and a broad product platform covering Life and General Insurance, MPF, and investment funds. 我們的理財顧問配備完善支援，能夠為客戶提供專業、合適且以目標為導向的理財策劃建議，並以客戶為本的理念為依歸，結合涵蓋人壽及一般保險、MPF及投資基金的多元產品平台。

Strong Support & Career Development培訓與指導

We provide structured professional training and mentorship programmes, together with a dynamic working environment that promotes flexibility, collaboration, and long‑term career development. 我們提供系統化的專業培訓及導師指導計劃，配合充滿活力的工作環境，促進靈活協作，並致力支持長遠的職業發展。

Career Path 事業發展路徑

FWD Financial Planning offers a clear and structured career pathway for financial advisors at different stages of their professional journey. FWD 財務策劃為不同階段的理財顧問提供清晰而有系統的事業發展路徑。

Financial Planning Advisor 理財策劃顧問

Build core advisory capabilities through client service, financial reviews and continuous professional development. Fresh graduates and IANG applicants are welcomed. 透過客戶服務、財務檢視及持續專業發展，建立穩固的顧問基礎。歡迎應屆畢業生及 IANG 人士加入。

Manager 經理

Develop people management skills, support junior advisors and expand client relationship management responsibilities. 發展人員管理能力，支援初級顧問，並擴展客戶關係管理職能。

Director 總監

Advance into senior advisory practice and leadership responsibilities, with greater strategic and team oversight, including opportunities for override incentives. 晉身高級顧問及領導層角色，負責更高層次的策略及團隊管理，並享有相關管理獎勵機會。

Executive Director 執行總監

Lead and grow advisory teams through business development, talent recruitment and team expansion, while mentoring future leaders and supporting long‑term growth through team‑based incentives. 透過業務拓展、人才招募及團隊發展，領導及壯大顧問團隊，同時培育未來領袖，並以團隊導向的激勵機制支持長遠發展。

Across all levels, advisors benefit from clear progression towards management roles, performance-based recognition and opportunities such as Million Dollar Round Table (MDRT) qualification. 各職級均設有清晰的管理晉升機制、以表現為本的認可制度，以及如百萬圓桌會（MDRT）等專業成就機會。
